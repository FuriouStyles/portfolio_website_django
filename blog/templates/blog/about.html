About Stephen

The Gist

I love to learn, build, and do cool stuff. Whether it's in a personal or professional capacity, I'm addicted to personal growth in all its forms; physically, intellectually, and spiritually. Currently, I am a student at Lambda School studying artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. I genuinely enjoy coming up with machine learning problems I can apply to areas in which I have deep passion, such as boxing or burritos. For example, I built a model that can predict with 73% accuracy the outcome of a boxing match. As far as burritos go, everyone knows you construct them in an elongated fashion so as to ensure all ingredients are equally represented in any cross-section of the burrito.
I wasn't always a super-awesome machine learning developer. Prior to my newfound passion for the next great industrial revolution, I specialized in business development consulting. My particular area of expertise was sales and building leading sales teams. Over almost a decade of business development experience, I've worked in international consulting, technology, commercial and residential real estate, and briefly as a proprietary trader. I've developed business plans for startups in consumer technology businesses, and I've launched a few of my own startups with varying degrees of success, though I haven't had the opportunity to apply my own technological prowess to any of my own startups. Yet.
When I'm not building cool stuff with my hands or my computer, I'm usually being an awesome dad or a great husband. I am an active amateur boxer, so I spend a lot of time training. I keep a close eye on the financial markets, and an even closer eye on my kitchen because I'm usually cooking something AMAZING. Besides my beautiful wife and two spectacular children, we also have two pretty super doggos that live with us as well. When I have time, I like to hit the billiards tables, but this isn't as often as I'd like it to be. Video games are also cool :) More...
